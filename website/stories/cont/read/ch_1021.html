1021 Episode 62 Snowfield Kim Dokja (11)

The moment the moonlight finally touched Cha Seongwoo's feet, archangels stepped forward as if to protect him. The moonlight enveloped the archangels.

Just as the pale death sentence fell, Cha Yerin screamed.

"No!"

No matter how great they were as archangels, they were ultimately 'narrative-grade constellations'. There was no way a narrative-grade could handle the power of a Demon God comparable to a myth-grade constellation.

It was Ye Hyunwoo who grabbed Cha Yerin as she tried to run toward her brother.

"Let go of me!"

"Yerin-ah, calm down."

Holding her, Ye Hyunwoo calmly pointed forward and spoke.

"Seongwoo is not dead."

Ye Hyunwoo's words were true.

Even in the catastrophic moonlight that shatters the stories of all beings, the Archangels remained intact. Although the outer surfaces of their wings were scorched and their fluttering feathers had disintegrated into particles, the Archangel formation did not collapse. A gentle curtain of light enveloped Cha Seongwoo and the Archangels.

「 The Archangels, who are narrative-grade constellations, have absorbed the power of the Demon God. 」

It was impossible by common sense. No 'narrative-grade' can stand against a 'myth-grade'. But now, the Archangels had made that impossibility a reality.

"Ji Eunyu."

Cha Seongwoo revealed himself once again between the Archangels' wings. He was speaking while gazing at the moon in the sky.

"Have you never thought about that?"

Was it a delusion? At that moment, Cha Seongwoo's voice as he opened his mouth did not sound like a human's.

"How did <Eden>, which lacks the number of 'myth-grade constellations' compared to other nebulae, become the 'Great Nebula' that everyone fears?"

Although <Eden> did possess the myth-grade Metatron and the comparable Michael, the number of 'myth-grade constellations' was still small compared to other nebulae.

"<Eden> does not need a 'Great Star'."

Since when had it begun? The archangels were clasping their hands together. Centered around Metatron at the rear, all the archangels were praying.

[The 'Giant Story' of the Great Nebula, <Eden>, begins its storytelling!]

The archangels' prayers gathered, and the story soon condensed into a single point.

There stood the Acting President of <Eden>.

Radiance surged from Cha Seongwoo's entire body, as if he had become a single star.

The moment the pale moonlight shone upon the archangels once again, Cha Seongwoo's right hand pointed toward the sky.

「 The greatest light of <Eden> is the very fact that they are 'right'. 」

Pointing at the moon with a movement that was neither too fast nor too slow, Cha Seongwoo recited the first line of a very ancient story.

"Let there be light."

And the vision of all beings was instantly plunged into darkness. It was a fleeting moment, yet a darkness reminiscent of eternity. And at the end of that darkness, a blinding light burst forth.

Angels, incarnations, and even demon kings were overwhelmed by that light. Some shed tears, while others felt an intense longing.

Ji Eunyu was no exception. Feeling a terrible emptiness, Ji Eunyu could not take her eyes off that light.

All they could do was stare blankly at the spot where the light had vanished like magic.

A huge hole had opened in the sky where the sublime light had swept through.

No one could accurately understand the cause and effect of that scene, but everyone clearly understood the result.

[The Outer God 'Falling Moon' exited the scenario after suffering a near-zero blow.]

A miracle that annihilated a myth-grade Demon God with a single strike. Before that immense power, all Demon Kings fell silent.

Except for Agares alone.

[Oh, <Eden>. Did you dare use that power...]

He knew the name of that light.

「 The Radiance of Creation—'Prima Lux'. 」

It was the light that opened the very beginning of <Eden>. That light was <Eden>'s confidence, and the reason they had no need for other 'myth-grade constellations'.

Because with this single story, they were able to defeat all 'myth-grade constellations'.

[The story, 'Commander of the Battlefield', continues its storytelling.]

[Exclusive Trait, 'Game Expert', activates!]

As Cha Seongwoo's story and trait manifested simultaneously, the morale-boosted angels began to move again.

[Kill the demons!]

The archangels linked to him also resumed the war.

[Annihilate evil.]

Ruthless good began to slaughter evil.

At the center of that stage, Cha Seongwoo looked like the Messiah of this world. Everyone in the <Star Stream> was watching him as if entranced.

However, only Ji Eunyu gazed at him with sorrowful eyes.

"Why."

The sleeve of Cha Seongwoo's right arm fluttered as he activated the 'Radiance of Creation'.

The 'Giant Story' of Creation, which a single human could not bear.

His ■■ was confirmed due to the transfer of the previous war. Now that this fight is over, Cha Seongwoo's soul will be destroyed in the aftermath.

"Do you think he will acknowledge you if you protect him to this extent?"

"I don't need such a thing as acknowledgment."

Cha Seongwoo's chilling warning followed.

"And there is one more arm left."

The Radiance of Creation was gathering at the tip of his left hand.

"Step back. If you come any closer, I will open fire."

Even Ji Eunyu had no way to counter a power capable of returning even a myth-grade demon to the void.

Nevertheless, instead of backing down, Ji Eunyu took a step closer to Cha Seongwoo and spoke.

"The author once said something like that while crying."

"Do not come near..."

"Editor-nim, what about a certain manuscript? No matter how much I revise it, it just won't write the way I want."

Ji Eunyu recalled the expression on the face of the writer in charge as he said those words. The man who had squeezed out tears and snot while staring at the blinking cursor.

「 Some stories turn even the writer who wrote them into a reader. 」

Looking at that face, Ji Eunyu thought.

If even the writer cannot control this story, where on earth does it come from? Who is forcing him to write this story? By completing this story, what do the writer who wrote it, the reader who reads it, the protagonist living it, and we ultimately become?

"So, what did that guy do?"

The reader asked, and the editor answered.

"He wrote the next chapter while crying."

"…"

"Because that was all he could do."

Ji Eunyu smiled broadly.

The record resumed at her fingertips.

「 That story began in the 'subway'. 」

Reading Ji Eunyu's sentence, Cha Seongwoo frowned and said.

"That record cannot continue. You must know, right?"

Instead of answering, Ji Eunyu looked up at the sky.

[The Chaos level of the battlefield is rising!]

The sky was blinking violently, as if flashing.

[The Chaos level of the battlefield is rising!]

The Chaos level was rising endlessly. Due to the clash between good and evil reaching its peak, stories of chaos were overflowing.

Looking up at that chaotic sky, Ji Eunyu recalled the moment she first obtained a sponsor.

「 "What is the reason I should make you my sponsor?" 」

On the day she received the offer to choose 'Lotus Blooming Under the Moonlight' as a sponsor, Ji Eunyu asked that.

「 "If I choose you, what can you do for me?" 」

[The constellation, 'Lotus Blooming Under the Moonlight', says it can offer advice regarding the scenario.]

「 "But I already know a lot about the scenario myself?" 」

Strictly speaking, her sponsor, who came from another timeline, was incompetent.

Constrained by the limitations of probability, her sponsor could not provide her with great support, and it was difficult to provide information that she did not know at all.

Nevertheless, she chose that patron star.

[The constellation, 'Lotus Blooming Under the Moonlight', has become your sponsor.]

It was all for today.

「 There, a soldier who wanted to be righteous stood by. 」

Sentences that were thought to never continue began to be written.

Just as Cha Seongwoo, with his eyes wide open, was about to open his mouth to say something, Metatron spoke.

[Shoot her.]

At the Archangel's chilling command, Cha Seongwoo shook his head violently.

"I cannot do that."

[If you do not, I will.]

Metatron's massive hand grabbed Cha Seongwoo's arm. With the sensation that his entire joint was being twisted, his left arm was forcibly moved.

"Let go of me! I am the acting president of <Eden>!"

[If you do not shoot her now.]

Cha Seongwoo's left arm trembled, overwhelmed by Metatron's power. He manifested his power to resist time and time again, but Metatron suppressed even that and spoke.

[This world will perish.]

"No!"

Despite Cha Seongwoo's desperate resistance, his arm eventually aimed at Ji Eunyu.

"Dodge!"

The prayers of the Archangel were gathering. Looking at Cha Seongwoo's left arm aimed at her, Ji Eunyu recalled the story she loved.

It was the third 'Giant Story' of <Kim Dokja Company>.

['Stage' is activated!]

[The Giant Story, 'Season of Light and Darkness', resonates with your stage.]

Her sponsor was watching over her story.

[The constellation, 'Lotus Blooming Under the Moonlight', gazes upon you.]

Watching the overflowing story, Cha Seongwoo, in a desperate voice, shouted.

"I know what you're thinking! You intend to raise the 'Chaos Level' to summon <Kim Dokja Company>, don't you!"

Currently, this place was temporarily set up as the stage for the 'Holy-Demon War'.

And the 'Holy-Demon War' was one of <Kim Dokja Company's> main battlefields.

"But even for the 'Holy-Demon War', we cannot summon all of them!"

Cha Seongwoo grasped reality more coldly than anyone else.

"If we could summon the entire <Kim Dokja Company> with this level of probability, they would have descended into this world long ago!"

Ji Eunyu knew it too.

There was probably only one member of <Kim Dokja Company> capable of descending into this battlefield.

And the being with the highest probability of descending was her sponsor.

"Your sponsor alone is impossible! The Moonlight Empress alone cannot stop this!"

Nevertheless, Ji Eunyu did not stop recording.

[It is time to let you go.]

With Metatron's command, Cha Seongwoo's left arm radiated light.

[Let there be light.]

With the incantation of a mythical archangel, a merciless barrage of light swept through everything before their eyes.

「 No matter who comes from <Kim Dokja Company>, they cannot withstand that barrage. 」

Cha Seongwoo despaired. His whole body trembled. He hadn't come all the way here just to witness a story like this.

"Metatron!"

Angrily, Cha Seongwoo shouted at Metatron. However, Metatron was not looking at him.

[The constellation, 'Scribe of Heaven', sighs and falls silent.]

Metatron had remained calm even when the Demon Kings, including Agares, descended, and even when the Demon God appeared.

Yet, for the first time, Metatron's expression had hardened.

As the halo of light disappeared, Cha Seongwoo looked back at the battlefield.

「 ...There was a man. 」

Ji Eunyu's record had been cut off, unable to continue to the end. However, the moment he read that sentence again, Cha Seongwoo realized something.

It was not that the record had been left unfinished.

「 There was the strongest and loneliest man in the world, 」

The record was intended to be written only up to that point from the very beginning.

"No way."

The moment he saw the tail of a pitch-black coat fluttering through the halo of light, Cha Seongwoo got goosebumps.

「 He was the figure he wanted to emulate more than anyone else in this world. 」

The dark blade, having deflected the radiance of creation, aimed at them with a haughty aura.

It was merely a sword pointing in this direction, yet the oppressive weight felt as if an entire world were turning against them.

At the mere sight of that presence, a certain archangel recoiled, and a certain demon king turned his back and began to flee.

The moment Cha Seongwoo looked into the man's eyes, he understood why the 'Radiance of Creation' had not worked on him.

「 He is the protagonist born with the 'beginning' of this world. 」

As all the stars on the battlefield trembled in silence, Cha Seongwoo whispered his name softly.

[Incarnation, 'Yoo Joonghyuk', descends as the calamity of 'Ragnarok'!]

The Supreme King of the 1,864th round has finally revealed himself in the 41st round of the <Star Stream>.
