People were gathered in a pure white space resembling a snowfield. They were people who had guarded a single world for a very long time.
Watching people take their seats one by one, Yoo Sangah counted in her head. One, two, three... At some point, the numbers stopped going up. How high was the number originally supposed to go? Or, how high was the number capable of going?
Yoo Sangah could not remember.
"Everyone is here, Sangah-ssi."
At Lee Seolhwa's words, Yoo Sangah looked up. As she slowly looked around, she saw the faces of the people who had been nothing more than numbers in her head. They were people she could recognize no matter where they were in this universe.
<&>「But their presence was definitely weaker than before.」
Deep shadows cast over their eyes. An indescribable weariness was etched on every face. It was the price for recklessly traversing worldlines and twisting the probability of other universes.

"Unnie."

"Please make a decision quickly. I am ready to leave right now."
The time to call her a girl had passed. Nevertheless, Yoo Sangah clearly remembered the face of the girl who had slayed Specters in Chungmuro.
"The time to worry about probability has already passed."
Lee Jihye is now called the 'Monarch of the Great Sea'. She has become such a formidable incarnation that even those fearsome outer deities make way for her ghost fleet. It was not just Lee Jihye.
"We’ve come this far, so there is nothing to be afraid of. We don't have much time left anyway. And above all—"
The voice of a boy who had gone through puberty. The boy, now with a fairly masculine jawline, was speaking.
"I have entered that world."
Every time the boy spoke, a hallucinatory sound of a swarm of insects crying could be heard from somewhere. It was likely the Demon God 'Abaddon's'.
The will of the ancient Demon God, who still protected the boy, was responding to his courage.
"I am confident I can enter it again and again."
Lee Gilyoung had already nearly died once in the 41st round. Falling into Asmodeus's scheme, he almost lost his life on a manipulated stage. Nevertheless, Lee Gilyoung said he would make the same choice once again.
Yoo Sangah, and everyone else present, knew why Lee Gilyoung was acting so strongly.
"We have to find Shin Yoosoung."

The other members of <Kim Dokja Company> also seemed to agree. They had already lost precious comrades several times over. And if they delayed any longer here, they might lose another comrade.
"Right now—"
The moment Lee Gilyoung sprang to his feet, an intense ringing in the white space echoed. A ringing that seemed to come from a very distant place. It sounded like a baby crying, or perhaps someone muttering.
"Did you just hear that?"
As Lee Gilyoung spoke, the other members of <Kim Dokja Company> nodded as well.
"Something is calling us."
Lee Seolhwa activated the central control unit as she spoke. Then, the coordinates of the location from which the crying came appeared on the large screen.
Lee Jihye asked.
"Which way is it? 'The 41st round'?"

The crying was heard once again. This time, it was clearer. Listening closely, it wasn't crying, but a story.
<&>「...Surviving in a ruined world…」
Long, echoing sentences. At that moment, everyone at <Kim Dokja Company> realized.
<&>「...There are three ways…」
Just what kind of being was calling them from beyond.
<&>「As if entranced, everyone there gazed in the same direction.」

"Can we go there?"
Lee Seolhwa nodded. The next moment, a shimmering portal appeared next to the central control unit.
Tsutsutsutsu.
A passageway where the aftershocks of probability were raging. Passing through that passage would lead to where the story had come from.
Lee Seolhwa spoke again.
"But safety..."
"I told you. The time for nitpicking about such things has passed."
The moment Lee Gilyoung walked toward the portal without hesitation, a large hand grabbed the boy's shoulder.
"Stop."

"You must not go."
A low but heavy voice. Just as Lee Gilyoung was about to speak again in defiance, Yoo Joonghyuk added.

Lee Jihye stepped forward and spoke.
"Um, I don't know if I'm mistaken, but..."

The Demon King of Salvation. The Watcher of Light and Darkness. The Prisoner of the Golden Cage. And the Oldest Dream.

"Make your decision."

Yoo Sangah could not know. Because she was not a writer. She was merely a friend to the readers. Someone who listens to the readers' impressions and listens to their words. Someone who converses with the readers.
But here, there were neither readers nor writers. What was here now was—
<@>[The 'Recorder of Fear', who has signed a 'Record Contract' with you, is calling you.]

"Sangah-ssi?"
As Yoo Sangah’s silence grew longer, Lee Seolhwa called out to her with an anxious expression. However, Yoo Sangah remained silent for quite some time. She simply read the record before her eyes over and over again. How much time had passed like that? A determination flickered in Yoo Sangah’s eyes.
