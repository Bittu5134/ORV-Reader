"Anyway, he was an extraordinary friend from the start. While everyone else was out of their minds, he alone was fully alert and catching the bugs."
Perhaps it was due to the middle-aged man's masterful storytelling skills. The constellations reacted immediately.
"Bugs! That's a brilliant strategy!"

Even the constellations, who had been skeptical at first, were soon engrossed in the story. I, the Great Sage, Heaven’s Equal, and Jung Heewon also joined the crowd to listen.

"So? Did that guy tell you?"

The middle-aged man continued his story. The time they joined forces to catch the bugs, taught the people the strategy, and tried to survive the 'First Scenario' together.
"Humans wouldn't do such a thing! Don't lie!"

"But then, that cult leader bastard appeared!"

The appearance of the Life Cult Leader Kim Cheolyang. The debate over whether bacteria are living organisms or not. The birth of a cult. Meeting Lee Cheoldu, the boss of the Cheoldu faction. Even the change in the scenario completion conditions just before the scenario ended. And finally.

The atmosphere in the dining hall fell solemn.
This was because none of the constellations here failed to understand what it meant for the scenario's time limit to have expired.
"Thanks to that friend, I am alive here right now."
The constellations who had been pointing fingers at the middle-aged man, claiming his story was a lie, as well as those who had been constantly nodding their heads or engaging in heated debates, remained silent for that moment.
Then someone asked.
"So, did that guy, that friend, end up dead?"
Friend. I remember the person who had called me that for a long time. The person who had called me friend from the very beginning.
He was speaking.
"No, my friend did not die."
At that, the reactions of the constellations exploded.
"How did he survive? Didn't he fail to kill a single living creature?"
"A watching constellation must have used a Deus Ex Machina for him!"
"What nonsense! If he had done that in the first scenario—"
Despite the boisterous reactions of the constellations, the middle-aged man simply smiled silently. He seemed accustomed to this scene.
It was then that Odin, who had remained silent the entire time, raised his hand. As the middle-aged man pointed at him, Odin asked.
"S-so, what is that friend of yours doing right now?"
At the question, the middle-aged man's expression darkened for the first time. He hesitated for a while, then answered with a faint smile.
"He is alive."
Alive. A voice filled with faint but clear conviction.
"Surely, he must be alive somewhere."
Muttering this as if in prayer, the middle-aged man soon clapped lightly and brought order to the room.
"That is all for today's story. See you again at the next story reading. Now, let's eat!"
The distribution then began. The constellations lined up in turn to receive their rations. Today's menu was spicy stir-fried pork made with frozen pork belly.
"Oh, this is—"
"So this is frozen pork belly!"
I sat among them and ate with them. The vegetables and fruits grown in the smart farm were so fresh that I found myself wanting another bowl without even realizing it.
"Of course it's delicious. After all, Demeter harvested it herself."
As the Great Sage Equal to Heaven muttered, an old woman who had been eating silently in the distance looked over at me with an embarrassed expression.
'Demeter,' the ruler of agriculture and the seasons.
It seemed she, one of the Twelve Olympians, was also here.
With a dazed heart, I swallowed a strawberry and said,
"It's delicious."
A world where myth-grade constellations farm and cook their own meals. Did Kim Dokja really dream of a world like this? If so, what did he want to see at the end of this world?
Just then, a middle-aged man who had just received his meal ration approached our table and sat down.
"Son-ssi, have you arrived?"
"Yes."
"Heewon-ssi, you've arrived too."
The man looked at Jung Heewon beside him, then glanced around again as if searching for someone else.
"Didn't Hyunsung-ssi come with you?"
"Yeah, he's a bit busy."
Busy. Is it really okay to describe it that simply? I thought of Lee Hyunsung, who must be struggling, surrounded by men in black suits right now. Suddenly, I wondered if it was okay for me to be eating something like this here.
"Excuse me."
It was then that Jung Heewon spoke. Ahjussi replied.
"Yes, please go ahead."

"Ah."

"Surely not..."

"Just as much as you wished for me to be alive, I too wished for you to be alive."
Perhaps that is the story we truly wished for each other.
"Yes. I give."
He put down his chopsticks with trembling hands and looked at me.
He was the first person I met in this world, 'Kim Dokja: Jiyoon's father, and my old friend.'
"It's been a long time, Dansu ahjussi."
Amidst the bustling conversation of the rural constellations, I could see the tablecloth on his table getting wet.
I started eating again. Then, Dansu ahjussi picked up his chopsticks again and ate. We remained silent until we had finished our bowls.
It was a meal with Dansu ahjussi, whom I had met again after eight years.
